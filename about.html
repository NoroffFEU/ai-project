About App

Welcome to our innovative student project, where we bring you an AI-assisted Daily Task List designed to enhance your productivity and motivation levels. Our app aims to revolutionize the way you approach your daily tasks by incorporating artificial intelligence to provide personalized tips and motivational messages, empowering you to achieve your goals efficiently and effectively.

Our team of passionate students embarked on this journey to create a tool that goes beyond the conventional task management apps.

Uses must Login to access "Your Week" that displays all tasks for week and not just the current day, for easy access to view and add tasks.

What sets our project apart is its collaborative nature. As part of our commitment to continuous improvement, we plan to hand over the development of the app from one class to the next. This ensures that the app evolves with fresh ideas, new features, and enhanced functionality, driven by the creativity and dedication of future students.
