About App

Welcome to our innovative student project, where we bring you an Al-assisted Daily Task List designed to enhance your productivity and motivation levels. Our app aims to revolutionise the way you approach your daily tasks by incorporating artificial intelligence to provide personalised tips and motivational messages, empowering you to achieve your goals efficiently and effectively. Our team of passionate students embarked on this journey to create a tool that goes beyond the conventional task management apps. With our Al bot at the helm, you can expect tailored recommendations and encouraging messages throughout your day, helping you stay focused, inspired, and on track. What sets our project apart is its collaborative nature. As part of our commitment to continuous improvement, we plan to hand over the development of the app from one class to the next. This ensures that the app evolves with fresh ideas, new features, and enhanced functionality, driven by the creativity and dedication of future students.
