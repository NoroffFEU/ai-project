How to Use AIMOT

Your complete guide to mastering daily task management with AIMOT

Welcome to AIMOT!

AIMOT is your personal daily planning assistant designed to help you stay organized and motivated. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started.

Use the links below to navigate to specific topics, or scroll down to read through the complete guide.

Adding Tasks Viewing Tasks Editing Tasks AI Assistant

1 Adding Tasks

Create and organize your daily tasks in just a few simple steps.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Locate the Task Input Field

    Find the "Add New Task" input field in your daily planner.

  2. Enter Your Task

    Type a clear, concise description of what you need to accomplish.

  3. Click "Add Task" or Press Enter

    Your task will instantly appear in your daily task list.

Pro Tip: Break large tasks into smaller, actionable steps for better progress tracking!

2 Viewing Tasks

Access and review your tasks.

Viewing Options:

Daily View

See all tasks scheduled for today. This is your default view when you open AIMOT.

  • View incomplete tasks
  • See completed tasks

Weekly View

Navigate to "Your Week" to see a calendar view of all your tasks for the week.

  • Overview of the entire week
  • Track progress across days
  • Plan ahead effectively

Task Display Features:

  • Checkboxes: Mark tasks as complete
  • Color Categories: Organization by task type
  • Task Details: Full description visible

[Screenshot: Task list view]

3 Editing Tasks

Modify, update, or remove tasks as your day evolves.

Edit Task Details

  1. Click on the task you want to edit
  2. The task text becomes editable
  3. Make your changes
  4. Save your task

Mark as Complete

Simply click the checkbox next to any task to mark it as complete. Completed tasks will:

  • Show as completed
  • Be saved in your history (for logged-in users)

Delete a Task

  1. Find the task you want to remove
  2. Click the delete icons
  3. Confirm deletion if prompted

4 AI Assistant

Get personalized motivation, tips, and insights powered by AI.

What AIMOT Can Do:

Daily Motivation

Receive inspiring quotes and encouragement to start your day right.

Productivity Tips

Get practical advice on time management and task prioritization.

Progress Insights

Understand your productivity patterns and celebrate achievements.

How to Interact with AIMOT:

  1. Access the AI Assistant

    Look for the AIMOT chat icon in the bottom right corner of your screen.

  2. Ask Questions or Request Motivation

    Type your question, task or simply say "Motivate me!" to get started.

  3. Receive Personalized Responses

    AIMOT analyzes your prompt and gives advice.

  4. Apply Suggestions

    Use the insights to improve your planning and productivity.

Example Questions to Ask AIMOT:
  • "How can I prioritize my tasks today?"
  • "Give me a motivational quote"
  • "What's the best way to manage my time?"
  • "Help me break down this large project"
