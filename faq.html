Adding Tasks

Create and organize your daily tasks in just a few simple steps.

  1. Locate the Task Input Field: Find the "Add New Task" input field in your daily planner.
  2. Enter Your Task: Type a clear, concise description of what you need to accomplish.
  3. Click "Add Task": Your task will instantly appear in your daily task list.
Pro Tip: Break large tasks into smaller, actionable steps for better progress tracking!
2

Viewing Tasks

Access and review your tasks in daily or weekly view.

Daily View

See all tasks scheduled for today with incomplete and completed tasks.

Weekly View

Navigate to "Your Week" to see a calendar view of all your tasks.

3

Editing Tasks

Modify, update, or remove tasks as your day evolves.

  1. Click on the task you want to edit
  2. The task text becomes editable
  3. Make your changes
  4. Save your task
